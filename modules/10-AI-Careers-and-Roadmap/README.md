# Module 10 — AI Careers and Roadmap

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module students will:

- Identify AI-related career paths relevant to their discipline
- Know which certifications are most valuable for AI careers
- Prepare an AI-enhanced resume and LinkedIn profile
- Build a personal AI learning roadmap beyond this course

## Topics Covered

### 1. AI Career Landscape Overview
### 2. Roles: AI Engineer, Data Scientist, Prompt Engineer, ML Ops, AI Product Manager
### 3. AI in Non-Tech Careers (Law, Medicine, Finance, Marketing)
### 4. Key Certifications and Credentials
### 5. Building Your AI Portfolio
### 6. Resume and LinkedIn for AI Roles
### 7. Interview Preparation for AI Jobs
### 8. Your Personal AI Learning Roadmap

## Demonstrations

- Review of strong AI portfolios on GitHub
- LinkedIn profile optimization with Copilot
- Mock AI interview question walkthrough

## Hands-on Activities

- Update your resume to highlight AI skills gained in this course
- Identify 3 certifications to pursue in the next 6 months
- Create a 90-day personal AI learning plan

## Assignment

Build your AI Career Starter Pack:
1. Updated resume with AI skills section
2. LinkedIn "About" section rewritten with Copilot
3. 3-month learning roadmap with specific goals and resources

# Module 09 — Future of AI

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module students will:

- Identify emerging AI technologies and trends
- Understand the concept of AGI and its implications
- Analyse how AI is transforming key industries
- Form an informed perspective on the long-term future of AI

## Topics Covered

### 1. Where AI Is Headed — Key Trends
### 2. Multimodal AI — Text, Image, Audio, Video Together
### 3. AI Agents and Autonomous Systems
### 4. Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) — What It Means
### 5. AI and the Future of Work
### 6. Industry Disruption — Healthcare, Education, Finance, Law
### 7. AI and Climate / Sustainability
### 8. Human-AI Collaboration

## Demonstrations

- Demo of AI agents completing multi-step tasks autonomously
- Exploring multimodal models (GPT-4o, Gemini)
- AI-generated video overview

## Hands-on Activities

- Research one industry and map out how AI will change 3 specific roles
- Explore an AI agent tool and document what it can and cannot do
- Group discussion: "What should AI never be allowed to do?"

## Assignment

Create a "Future of AI in [Your Industry]" brief — a 1-page summary of how AI will reshape your target career field over the next decade, with your own recommendations.

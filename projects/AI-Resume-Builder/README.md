# Project: AI Resume Builder

**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Estimated time:** 3–5 hours
**Tools:** ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, or any LLM

---

## Problem Statement

Job applications are stressful, and writing a compelling resume is a skill most students haven't been taught. Many students undersell their experience by using weak, vague language.

This project uses AI to transform raw experience into a polished, professional resume — while keeping the student in control of accuracy and authenticity.

---

## What You'll Build

A complete, personalised resume package including:
1. A one-page resume with AI-enhanced bullet points
2. A cover letter template adaptable to 3 job types
3. A LinkedIn "About Me" section
4. An interview preparation Q&A document (10 likely questions + your answers)

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1 — Gather Your Raw Material (30 min)
Write down (rough notes, no need to be perfect):
- Your education (degree, college, year, relevant coursework)
- Any work experience, internships, or part-time jobs
- Projects you've worked on (college or personal)
- Skills (technical and soft)
- Extracurriculars, volunteering, leadership roles
- Certifications or online courses completed

### Step 2 — AI-Enhanced Resume Bullets (45 min)
For each experience, paste your rough notes and use this prompt:
```
You are a professional resume writer. Rewrite these rough experience notes as 
3 strong resume bullet points for a [your year] year [your course] student 
applying for [target role] at a technology company.
Use strong action verbs. Quantify impact where possible. Max 20 words each.

My notes: [paste your rough notes]
```

Collect all bullet points and organise into a resume template.

### Step 3 — Build the Resume (30 min)
Use this prompt to generate a complete resume structure:
```
Create a clean, ATS-friendly resume structure for a [your degree] student.
Sections: Summary, Education, Experience, Projects, Skills, Certifications.
Use the following bullet points I've already written: [paste your bullets]
```

Review carefully — edit for accuracy and personalisation.

### Step 4 — Cover Letter Template (30 min)
```
Write a 3-paragraph cover letter template for a [your degree] student 
applying for [role type] positions. 
Paragraph 1: Opening — why I'm excited about this role
Paragraph 2: Why I'm a strong candidate (leave [EXPERIENCE] placeholders)
Paragraph 3: Closing — call to action
Keep it under 250 words. Professional but not stiff.
```

### Step 5 — LinkedIn About Section (20 min)
```
Write a 250-word LinkedIn "About" section for a final-year [degree] student.
Tone: professional but approachable.
Include: what I'm studying, what I'm passionate about, key skills, what I'm looking for.
My background: [paste 3-4 sentences about yourself]
```

### Step 6 — Interview Prep (45 min)
```
I'm a [degree] student interviewing for [role type] internships.
Generate 10 likely interview questions and ideal answer frameworks for each.
Include: 3 technical questions, 4 behavioural questions (STAR format), 3 questions about AI skills.
```

---

## Deliverables

- [ ] Resume (PDF, 1 page)
- [ ] Cover letter template (Word/Google Doc)
- [ ] LinkedIn About section (text document)
- [ ] Interview Q&A document (10 questions with answer frameworks)
- [ ] Reflection (1 page): What did AI do well? What did you have to fix? Would you trust it fully?

---

## Extension Tasks

- Apply the resume to a real job listing and ask AI: *"Review my resume against this job description and suggest improvements"*
- Generate a skills gap analysis: *"Based on this job description, what skills am I missing and how can I develop them in 3 months?"*

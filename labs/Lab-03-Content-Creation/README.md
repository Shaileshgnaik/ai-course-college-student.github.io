## Part 1: Blog Post Generation (15 minutes)

**Task:** Generate a 400-word blog post on a topic relevant to your field of study.

**Step 1 — Write your prompt:**
```
You are a content writer for a student technology blog. 
Write a 400-word blog post titled "[Your Topic Here]" aimed at first-year college students.
Use a friendly, conversational tone. Include:
- An engaging opening paragraph
- 3 key points with short explanations
- A closing paragraph with a call to action
```

## Part 2: Resume Bullet Points (15 minutes)

AI is excellent at transforming weak resume descriptions into compelling ones.

**Task:** Improve these weak resume bullet points using AI.

Original bullets:
```
- Helped with customer service
- Did data entry tasks
- Was part of a project team
- Learned Python programming
- Used Microsoft Office
```

**Prompt to use:**
```
You are a professional resume writer. Rewrite these 5 resume bullet points 
for a final-year student applying for a technology internship. 
Use strong action verbs, include impact where possible, and keep each under 20 words.

[paste the bullet points]
```

Compare the original and improved versions. Which tells a better story?

## Part 3: Professional Email Drafting (15 minutes)

**Scenario:** You want to email a company asking for a 2-week internship opportunity during your semester break.

**Step 1 — Draft it yourself first** (5 minutes, no AI):
Write a rough draft of the email in your notebook.

**Step 2 — Use AI to refine it:**
```
You are a career advisor helping a student write a professional internship request email.
The student is a [your year] year [your course] student at [your college name].
They want to request a 2-week internship at [company name] in the [field] department.
Write a concise, professional email. Subject line included. Under 150 words.
```

**Step 3 — Compare:**
- What did your draft have that AI missed?
- What did AI do better than your draft?
- What would you change before sending the AI version?

---

## Part 4: Research Summarisation (15 minutes)

**Task:** Paste a paragraph from a research paper, news article, or textbook chapter into the AI and ask it to summarise and explain it.

**Prompt:**
```
Read the following text and:
1. Summarise it in 3 bullet points (max 15 words each)
2. Explain it as if I'm a first-year student with no background in this topic
3. List 2 questions a curious student might ask after reading this

[paste your text here]
```

**Evaluate the summary:**
- Is it accurate?
- Is it simpler and easier to understand than the original?
- Did the AI miss anything important?
- Would you use this technique when studying?

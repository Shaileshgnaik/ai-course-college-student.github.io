# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this course a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions
- Give constructive feedback; accept feedback graciously
- Credit others' work and ideas appropriately
- Use AI tools responsibly and ethically

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Submitting AI-generated work as entirely your own without disclosure (where required)
- Sharing course materials beyond the class without permission
- Disrupting sessions or lab activities

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the course trainer directly.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal from course activities or escalation to institutional authorities.

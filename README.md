# 🚀 AI Course for College Students
### From AI Fundamentals to Generative AI and Microsoft Copilot

Welcome to the **AI Course for College Students**, a comprehensive hands-on learning program designed to help students understand, explore, and apply Artificial Intelligence in academics, projects, and future careers.

This course combines theory, demonstrations, hands-on activities, and mini projects to provide a strong foundation in modern AI technologies including Machine Learning, Generative AI, Prompt Engineering, and Microsoft Copilot.

---

# 📋 Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence is transforming every industry, from healthcare and education to finance and software development.

This course helps students:

✅ Understand AI fundamentals

✅ Learn Machine Learning concepts

✅ Explore Deep Learning and Neural Networks

✅ Master Generative AI tools

✅ Develop Prompt Engineering skills

✅ Use Microsoft Copilot effectively

✅ Build AI-assisted applications

✅ Learn Responsible AI practices

✅ Explore AI career opportunities

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

- Explain core AI concepts and terminology
- Differentiate AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning
- Understand how Large Language Models (LLMs) work
- Create effective prompts for AI systems
- Use Microsoft Copilot and other AI tools productively
- Apply AI in research, learning, coding, and content creation
- Identify ethical considerations when using AI
- Design simple AI-powered solutions for real-world problems

---

# 👨‍🎓 Target Audience

This course is designed for:

- Engineering Students
- BCA / MCA Students
- B.Sc Computer Science Students
- Business and Management Students
- Faculty Members
- Technology Enthusiasts
- Beginners Interested in AI

No prior AI experience is required.
